Problem Statement

Magium is lacking voice beyond plain text.

Key Points and Goals:

Audience

Both readers and gamers; both nostalgics and newbies. The goal is to be accessible and appeal to a wide variety. By nature, a digital C.Y.O.A. R.P.G. story crosses audiences. As such it has the potential and desire to tap into these available audiences.

Best of Both Sides

Both readers and gamers; both nostalgics and newbies. Bring in familiar flavor for these dualities. I aim to bring people back for the paid book chapters by adding voice, flavor, and life to the experience.

Unleash the Imagination

Still aiming to leave the power of imagination with the user as is the original vision for the app. Everything is going towards further unleashing the imagination.
